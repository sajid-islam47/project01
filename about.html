About Cumilla University Of Engineering & Technology(CoUET)

The Cumilla University Of Engineering & Technology(CoUET) is a private university located in Cumilla, Bangladesh. Here is a summary of the available information: Overview Type: Private university, sponsored by the Bangladesh Army Welfare Trust and affiliated with the Bangladesh . Established: October, 15, 2025. Affiliation: Approved by the Ministry of Education and the University Grants Commission (UGC) of Bangladesh. Motto: "Knowledge, Wisdom and Technology." Vision: To become a center of excellence in Science, Engineering, and Technology. Location: Located in a secure and serene environment at Cantonment, Adarsha Sadar, Cumilla, adjacent to the Dhaka-Chattogram Highway. The main campus operates from Cumilla Cantonment. Academics and Departments BAIUST offers various programs under four main faculties: Faculty of Electrical and Computer Engineering (FECE): Bachelor of Science in Electrical and Electronic Engineering (EEE) Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) Bachelor of Science (Hons) in Information and Communication Technology (ICT) Faculty of Civil Engineering (FCE): Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering (CE) School of Business: Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) Bachelor of Economics Faculty of Science and Humanities (FSH): Department of English Department of Law Department of Science & Humanities Facilities and Services Hostel: The university provides hostel facilities for both male (Male Hall, accommodating 196 students) and female (Female Hall, accommodating 144 students with 80 more seats planned from Fall 2025 session) students. Library: It has an automated central library with books and journals. Laboratories: Equipped laboratories are available for both engineering and non-engineering departments (e.g., Software Engineering Lab, Fluid Mechanics Lab). Healthcare: BAIUST provides free healthcare facilities, including an emergency room with a medical officer and nurse, and dedicated emergency transport. Advanced facilities are provided through an affiliated clinic, 'Mainamati Cantonment General Hospital'. Other: The campus includes a Students' Welfare Office (SWO), classrooms with projectors and smart boards, and a focus on skill development programs and industry-oriented learning. Governance BAIUST is directly controlled by the Bangladesh Army. The Chief of Army Staff is the Chairman of the Board of Trustees.
